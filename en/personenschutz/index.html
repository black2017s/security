Personal Protection, Bodyguard and Escort

Personal protection or bodyguard from NIK SECURITY means security for you – in a discreet and effective manner: personal protection or escort protection durring events, trips abroad or meetings. We offer you multilingual bodyguards in Munich and all over Europe. Your VIP individual bodyguards, accompanies you every step of the way in the background and only appears in an emergency case. Let us consult you free of charge on the subject about personal protection, bodyguard, VIP escort protection. The protection and safety of our special customers is our daily job.

NIK SECURITY serves special vulnerable people in public with our professional bodyguards. In doing so, we protect you and your family from third party attacks.

At NIK SECURITY, the bodyguards have police and military experience in governmental personnel protection. They are very well trained in the handling of weapons and protected vehicles in order to give you the kind of security that you deserve and expect.

NIK SECURITY bodyguards work with you to develop special concepts for the prevention of kidnappings and suspected spying activities. Our employees remain invisible and clear the surroundings after changes in locations, residences or work places.

At irregular times, we patrol the routes and schools. We determine whether there are suspicious people or vehicles in the vicinity of the property or whether they are spying on your home or your children’s school. In the event of a threat, you can count on the employee immediately. This gives you and your family extensive protection.

NIK SECURITY offers you professional personal protection and escort protection in Munich, which is always carried out with at least two bodyguards per protected person, so that you feel safe enough. For spying protection tasks, one bodyguard is sufficient.

Protective persons are persons who are on a public stage and are exposed to a hazard prognosis after a risk, or persons who are exposed to significant threats from other people due to special living conditions (e.g. in witness protection programs, victims of organized crime).

State of the art armored (specially protected) vehicles, which can only hardly be distiguished from regular vehicles, can be used to transport the guarded person.
